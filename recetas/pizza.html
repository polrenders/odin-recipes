Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish featuring a thin crust, savory tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and a variety of delicious toppings.

Ingredients

  • 500 g of wheat flour
  • 7 g of dry yeast
  • 325 ml of warm water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 200 g of tomato sauce
  • 250 g of shredded mozzarella
  • Optional toppings: pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, basil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, kneading until a smooth dough forms.
  3. Let the dough rise, covered with a damp cloth, for 1 hour until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
  5. Roll out the dough and place it on a pizza tray.
  6. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
