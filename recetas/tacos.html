Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of small hand-sized tortillas filled with a variety of ingredients like meat, vegetables, and salsa.

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 300 g of ground beef or chicken
  • 1 tablespoon of taco seasoning
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • Salsa and guacamole (optional)

Steps

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or chicken over medium heat until fully cooked.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and a small amount of water, stirring until well combined. Let simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos by adding the meat to each tortilla.
  5. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and any other desired toppings.
  6. Serve immediately with sour cream, salsa, or guacamole on the side.
